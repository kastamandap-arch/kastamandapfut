About Us

Where Football Culture Thrives

A nature-surrounded futsal haven in Kumari Marg, Kathmandu.

Kastamandap Futsal Academy is a beloved open-roof futsal ground in Kumari Marg, Kathmandu — beautifully surrounded by nature and offering a peaceful, refreshing atmosphere unlike any typical urban sports facility.

Whether organizing a weekend match with friends, training for a tournament, or simply enjoying the beautiful game under open skies — Kastamandap Futsal is your home ground.
